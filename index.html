A two-degree-of-freedom robotic arm utilizing servo motors for precise angular movement. The design incorporates shoulder and elbow joints controlled by microcontroller-based position feedback. Applications include basic pick-and-place operations, educational demonstrations of kinematics, and foundational robotics concepts with minimal complexity.
